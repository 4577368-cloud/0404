When designing AI Prompts for this project:
- **Objective Tone**: Avoid overly dramatic metaphors like "blood clot" (血栓). Use professional, objective terms like "Critical Bottleneck" (关键瓶颈) or "Conversion Obstacle" (转化障碍).
- **Clean Output**: Strictly forbid the output of internal thinking processes (e.g., `<think>...</think>`). The model should only output the final result.
- **Structured Markdown**: Ensure output is formatted with clean Markdown headers, lists, and bold text for readability.
- **No Mental Health/Medical Analogies**: Do not use terms that imply disease or medical conditions when describing business problems.
